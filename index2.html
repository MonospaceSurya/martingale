The Martingale Strategy

A mathematical approach to betting that ensures recovery of all losses with a single win

How It Works

The Martingale strategy is a progressive betting system where you double your stake after every loss. This method requires each bet to have odds of at least 2x (or 2.0), ensuring you win double your stake amount.

With just one win, you recover your entire cumulative loss and profit an amount equal to your initial stake. This elegant mathematical principle has been used by professional bettors for centuries.
